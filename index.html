Golden Rule Mandate in the African Nation (GR-MAN)

Community Transformation through Moral Restoration, Youth Empowerment & Peacebuilding

Project Title

Community Transformation through Moral Restoration, Youth Empowerment, Victim’s Care, and Peacebuilding in Southern Nigeria

Executive Summary

Golden Rule Mandate in the African Nation (GR-MAN) seeks partnership and funding support from different Organizations to implement a multi-tiered peacebuilding and moral renewal initiative aimed at countering genocide, ethnic and tribal conflicts, and poverty-driven violence across Africa, with pilot implementation in Nigeria.

The proposed initiative leverages community-based education, interfaith dialogue, youth empowerment, and sustainable development models to foster social cohesion, reduce inter-group tensions, and promote ethical leadership.

Rooted in the universal principle of the Golden Rule, the program focuse areas including peace, democracy and governance, poverty reduction, social cohesion, early conflict prevention, youth engagement, and locally led development.

Background and Problem Statement

  • Ethno-religious conflicts (Jos, Kaduna, Plateau)
  • Farmer–herder crises across the Middle Belt
  • Insurgent and extremist violence (Boko Haram, ISWAP)
  • Resource-based conflicts in the Niger Delta
  • Widespread youth unemployment and poverty
  • Moral decline including corruption and intolerance

Traditional security-only responses are insufficient. GR-MAN advances a ground-up moral reorientation, values education, and peacebuilding approach transcending ethnic, religious, and political divides.

Organizational Profile

  • Moral and values-based education
  • Peacebuilding and conflict resolution
  • Youth empowerment and leadership development
  • Community resilience initiatives
  • Humanitarian outreach
  • Poverty alleviation and skills development

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Human dignity
  • Non-violence
  • Respect for diversity
  • Accountability

Project Goal and Objectives

Overall Goal

To strengthen moral awareness, reduce conflict, and promote sustainable peace and development.

Specific Objectives

  1. Promote moral reorientation using the Golden Rule principle
  2. Prevent ethnic and tribal violence through dialogue
  3. Empower youth with livelihood skills
  4. Strengthen interfaith collaboration
  5. Build peace education and trauma healing capacity
  6. Establish Peace and Values Clubs

Proposed Activities

Moral Reorientation & Education

  • Golden Rule curriculum development
  • Community ethics seminars
  • School and faith-based workshops

Youth Empowerment

  • Vocational training
  • Entrepreneurship & financial literacy
  • Youth cooperatives & mentorship

Peacebuilding & Early Warning

  • Interfaith dialogue forums
  • Community peace councils
  • Rapid-response mediation teams

Target Beneficiaries

  • Youth (15–35)
  • Women and widows
  • Religious and traditional leaders
  • Ethnic minority groups
  • Schools and communities

Geographic Focus
