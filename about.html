Who We Are

Golden Rule Mandate in the African Nation (GR-MAN) is a faith-inspired humanitarian and peacebuilding organization focused on moral restoration, social justice, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

A peaceful Africa where ethical values, justice, and unity guide sustainable development.

Our Mission

To transform communities through moral education, conflict resolution, youth empowerment, trauma healing, and interfaith collaboration.
